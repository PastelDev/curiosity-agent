# Solarpunk Magitech RPG World - Synthesis Iteration 2 (Refined & Expanded)

## World Name: Verdant Aetheria (Merged Concord/Solara)

## Cosmology (Detailed)
- **Aetherial Veil & Solar Heart**: Mana source, fluctuations cause **Flux Events** (table below).
  Flux Table (Environmental Roll d20 daily in cities):
  | Roll | Effect | Strategic Impact |
  |------|--------|------------------|
  | 1-5  | Wild Magic Surge | Spells risk wild table; advantage for chaos casters |
  | 6-15 | Stable | Normal casting |
  | 16-20| Boon | +1 spell slot regain, EP double |

- **Planes Access**: Via crystal gates in cities. Verdant Nexus for growth rituals.

## History (Full Timeline with Hooks)
1. **Dawnweaving (-5000 AR)**: Gods spark races. Hook: Ancient god-artifacts in ruins.
2. **Arcane Zenith (-1000-0 AR)**: Megastructures pollute mana. Hook: Recover lost rune-tech.
3. **Mana Cataclysm (0 AR)**: Solar Flare, empires shatter. 90% loss.
4. **Reclamation (0-500 AR)**: Scavengers find crystals. **Elara Sunweaver** pioneers.
5. **Magitech Dawn (500-1000 AR)**: Vine Accord (800 AR), factories boom.
6. **Solarpunk Revolution (1000-1250 AR)**: Sustainable shift, living cities.
7. **Flux Prelude (1250+)**: Veil tears, beasts awaken. **Current Year: 1257 AR**.

Key Events:
- Crystal Awakening (512 AR): Village lit.
- Vine Uprising (post-1000): Vines gain sentience.

## Mechanics Prototype (DnD 5.5e+)
- **Mana Uncertainty**: Pre-cast d20 + Int/Cha vs spell level DC (e.g., lvl3=13). Fail: Half/weak, <DC-5: d100 Wild Table (vine explode to sunbeam).
- **Sunlight Exposure Points (EP)**: Gain 1/turn direct sun (races double). Spend: 5EP adv attack, 10EP extra action.
