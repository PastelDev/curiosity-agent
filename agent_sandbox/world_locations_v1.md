## Thornwild Frontier (Rural, Pop 50k scattered)
**Overview**: Wild leylines, nomadic camps, Sunken Ruins (pre-Cataclysm).
**Features**: Vine trains (solar rail, Flux derail risk), Grove Councils (voting games).
**Hooks**: Cult incursions, artifact hunts. Contrast: Pure nature vs city tech.

## Aquavine Depths (Secondary City, Pop 200k)
**Overview**: Coral reef polis, water-sun crystals (hydro-solar hybrid). Mer-elf/drow mix.
**Districts**: Bubble markets, kelp comms vines (underwater msgs), abyss vents (high Flux).
**Hooks**: Submerged Shadow Age vaults, tidal mana surges.
