## Game Mechanics (Full DnD 5.5e Adaptations)
**Mana Uncertainty Core**:
- Before spell: d20 + caster mod (Int/Cha/Wis) vs DC = spell level x2 +10 (cantrip=10, lvl9=28).
  | Result | Outcome |
  |--------|---------|
  | Nat20 | Empower (+1d6/lvl dmg, +duration) |
  | >=DC | Normal |
  | <DC | Weak (half dmg/effect) |
  | Nat1 or <DC-5 | Wild Surge (d100 table sample below) |

**Wild Surge d100 Sample** (10 entries):
1-10: Vine Explosion (10ft rad, 3d6 piercing), 11-20: Sunbeam ally heal, ..., 91-100: Mega Empower.

**Sunlight EP System**:
- Track personal EP pool (max 20). Gain: 1/turn direct sun (races x2). Spend: 5=Adv next roll, 10=Reaction cast, 15=Shield (absorb 20 dmg).
- Strategic: Day fights position for sun, night disadvantage.
