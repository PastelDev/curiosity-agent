**Racial Feats (Lvl4+)**:
- **Crystal Bond**: +1 AC sun, recharge spell slot.
- **Flux Embrace**: Adv on uncertainty rolls, +1d4 wild surge.

**Cultural Integration**:
- **Verdantspire**: Multi-race guilds (elves/dwarves dominate tech).
- **Thornwild**: Florakin/Vinekin communes.
- **Aquavine**: Crystalli mer-adapted subculture.
