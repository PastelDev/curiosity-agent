# Campaigns and Adventures Iteration 1 (3-Tier Arcs, Hooks from World Elements)

## Campaign Structure Overview
**Levels 1-20 Arc**: Modular, tie history (Cataclysm/Flux), locations (Verdantspire/Thornwild), races/NPCs/mechanics (Uncertainty/EP).
**Themes**: Magitech revolution tensions - urban harmony vs rural wild, Flux uncertainty risks.

## Arc 1: Crystal Heist (Lvls 1-5, Urban Intrigue)
**Premise**: Slum village needs unstable crystal from Elara's Heights tower. PCs scavengers hired by Glim.
**Key Locations**: Root Slums -> Vineways -> Crystal Heights.
**NPCs**: Glim (broker), Thornak (rebel contact), Elara (boss fight?).
**Mechanics Focus**: Stealth unc rolls (low DC), EP night penal, vine comm hacks.
**Plot Beats**:
1. Slum hook (DC12 Invest recruit).
2. Market intel (Pers DC14 Glim).
3. Vineways sabotage (Stealth DC15).
4. Tower heist (Arc DC16 puzzle, CR3 golem).
**Twists**: Crystal wild surge blackout city block. Reward: 500gp, unstable shard (item).
**Hooks Table d6**: 1 Rebel sidequest, 2 Flux trap, 3 Double-cross, etc.

## Arc 2: Vine Uprising (Lvls 6-12, Faction War)
**Premise**: Thornak rebels seize Whisperwood hub. PCs broker/choose sides.
**Locations**: Thornwild Grove -> Vineways battle -> Solar Plaza.
**NPCs**: Thornak/Sylva (rebels), Elara/Dagna (Concord), Korrak (wilds aid).
**Mechanics**: Flux Meter spikes (DC+), EP battles day/night, grapple mass vines.
**Beats**:
1. Grove council (Cha DC16 diplomacy).
2. Train ambush (CR5 vine behemoth).
3. Hub siege (mass combat, unc chain).
**Climax**: Control node (skill challenge).
**Rewards**: Vine Master feat, faction rep.

## Arc 3: Mana Schism War (Lvls 13-20, Epic)
**Premise**: Dragon awakens from Flux imbalance. Realign Veil with pantheon ritual.
**Locations**: Aquavine Depths -> Planes (Nexus), Verdantspire finale.
**NPCs**: All prior + gods' avatars, Lirael smuggle.
**Mechanics**: High DC unc (25+), EP artifacts, lair actions Flux Meter.
**Beats**:
1. Prophecy (Div DC20).
2. Shard hunt (plane hops).
3. Dragon lair (CR18, solar rad breath).
**Epic**: Ritual circle (group Arc/Wis 30 success).
**Rewards**: Legendary stabilizer, god boon.

**Integration**: Arcs chain (heist sparks uprising, war from Meter). Side quests races (Florakin famine), items craft.
