# Solarpunk Magitech RPG World - Synthesis Iteration 1 (From Tournament + Internal)

## Synthesized History (Merged)
**World Name: Aetherial Concord / Solara Bloom**
- **Cosmology**: Aetherial Veil channels mana from Solar Heart. Planes: Material, Verdant Nexus, Elemental Flux, Shadow Veil, Celestial Spires. Mana Flux: d20 uncertainty rolls.
- **Timeline** (Blended):
  1. Dawnweaving / Age of Dragons: Gods create, dragons hoard mana.
  2. Arcane Zenith / Arcane Industrial: Empires, megastructures, pollution crisis.
  3. Mana Cataclysm / Greening Start: Solar Flare, revolution begins.
  4. Reclamation / Magitech Dawn: Sun crystals discovered (Elara Sunweaver, 512 AR / 0 GR).
  5. Solarpunk Revolution (1000 AR+): Living cities, Vine Accord (800 AR).
  6. Current (1250 AR): Flux prelude, urban-rural tensions.
