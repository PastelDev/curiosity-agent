## Aquavine Depths
**Details**: Bioluminescent reefs, bubble habitats. Water crystals refract sun for power.
**Districts**: Abyss Markets (exotic pearls), Kelp Forges, Vent Labs (high Flux research).
**Travel**: Mer-mounts, bubble lifts.

**Inter-City Travel**:
- Verdantspire-Thornwild: Vine train (2 days, 10gp).
- Verdantspire-Aquavine: Portal gate (50gp, weekly).
